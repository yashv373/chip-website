Introducing ChipMIT

Introducing ChipMIT

Introducing ChipMIT

ChipMIT is a student-driven initiative focused on microelectronics, digital systems, and VLSI design. It’s a place for hands-on learning, innovation, and collaboration, empowering future tech leaders to explore the world of chip design and build impactful solutions.

Team Structure

TheBoard Team

TheBoardTeam

The Board is made up of the division head members who oversee the strategic direction of ChipMIT. They are responsible for high-level decision-making, planning, and ensuring the club stays aligned with its goals.

TheCore Team

TheCoreTeam

The Core Team consists of second-year members who handle the day-to-day operations of ChipMIT. They execute tasks, manage events, and ensure smooth implementation of projects.

TheFoundation Team

TheFoundation Team

The Foundation Team is made up of first-year members who support the club’s activities. They assist with tasks, help with publicity, and play an essential role in bringing projects to life
